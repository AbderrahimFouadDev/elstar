OUR PACKAGES

At ElStar Hostel,
we offer carefully designed stay packages to suit every traveler. Each option provides comfort,
flexibility, and a chance to enjoy the welcoming atmosphere of our hostel.
  • 7-Night Package: Ideal for a short getaway, giving you time to relax and experience the cozy ambiance of ElStar.
  • 10-Night Package: Perfect for those who want a longer stay, allowing you to settle in and fully enjoy the hostel’s vibrant environment.
  • 15-Night Package: Designed for travelers seeking an extended stay, offering the opportunity to truly feel at home and immerse in the unique spirit of ElStar.
No matter which package you choose, each stay at ElStar Hostel is designed to be comfortable, welcoming, and
unforgettable.

OUR ROOMS

ElStar Hostel offers a variety of rooms designed to blend comfort with Moroccan charm.
  • Double Room: Featuring a cozy double bed, elegant fabrics, and warm Moroccan accents, this room is
    perfect for couples or solo travelers seeking comfort and style.
  • Shared Room: Our shared dormitory accommodates 3 single beds, ideal for friends or solo adventurers.
    Each bed is designed with comfort and privacy in mind, while the room’s bohemian decor reflects the
    local craftsmanship.
Renovated with a unique spirit combining bohemian flair and Moroccan influences, ElStar’s rooms feature
captivating colors and artisanal details. Bright, elegant, and inviting, each space encourages you to unwind
and feel at home.

ACTIVITIES

Welcome to ElStar Hostel, where your stay is more than just a place to rest—it’s a journey into the
heart of Morocco. Ride the waves with our surf sessions, share the thrill of skateboarding, or find
calm and balance during yoga.
Explore breathtaking landscapes on a quad ride, wander the vibrant medina, visit the iconic surf
village of Taghazout, or glide down the golden dunes of Timlalin.
At ElStar, every experience is designed to create memories, stories, and connections that stay with
you long after your adventure ends.

Welcome

To Morocco, To Our Home

"Morocco, where mountains meet the ocean and journeys awaken the spirit"

Our hostel was born from the dream of young Moroccan adventurers — a place where travelers from all over the world can feel at home.

We created this place to welcome surfers, skaters, travelers, artists, and explorers from every corner of the world. Nestled just next to the mountain, it offers peace, fresh air, and stunning views that inspire calm and connection.

Here, you'll find more than a bed: you'll find warmth, authenticity, and the spirit of sharing that makes every journey unforgettable.
