Common Rules

We want El_Star_Hostel to feel like home for everyone. Please follow these simple rules to keep our space peaceful, friendly, and safe.

1. Quiet Hours

From 22:00 to 08:00.
Please respect others’ rest, especially in shared rooms.

2. Cleanliness

Keep shared areas and the kitchen tidy.
Wash your dishes after use.
Dispose of trash in bins provided.

3. Smoking & Alcohol

Smoking is not allowed inside rooms or indoor areas.
You may smoke in designated outdoor spaces.
Alcohol is allowed in moderation in common areas only.

4. Visitors

Only registered guests are allowed in dorms and private rooms.
Visitors are welcome in common areas until 22:00.

5. Safety

Keep valuables in provided lockers.
Always lock your room or locker when leaving.
Report any issue to staff immediately.

6. Respect

Be kind and respectful to guests, staff, and local culture.
Offensive, noisy, or disrespectful behavior will not be tolerated.

7. Pets

Pets are not allowed.

8. Activities

Activities like yoga, hiking, or surfing are at your own risk. Follow safety instructions and guides.
Thank you for helping us maintain a calm, social, and family-friendly hostel environment ❤
